Featured Attractions

Eiffel Tower

Experience the beauty of Paris from the iconic Eiffel Tower.

×

Local Tips for Eiffel Tower

Best time to visit: Early morning or late evening to avoid crowds.

Nearby attractions: Louvre Museum, Seine River.

Local Food

Try the classic French croissants and escargot, or indulge in a French onion soup.

Transport

The Paris Metro is the most convenient way to get to the Eiffel Tower. The nearest station is Trocadéro.

Cultural Tips

Be polite and greet with a "Bonjour" before speaking in English. French culture values good manners and respect for traditions.

×

Visitor Details for Eiffel Tower

Entry fee: €25 (Adults), €12 (Children).

Opening hours: 9 AM - 12 AM daily.

Taj Mahal

A symbol of love and India's rich history.

×

Local Tips for Taj Mahal

Best time to visit: Early morning for a serene experience.

Nearby attractions: Agra Fort, Mehtab Bagh.

Local Food

Try the famous Mughlai dishes like kebabs and biryanis. Don’t miss the Agra Petha, a sweet treat.

Transport

Agra is well connected by trains and buses. Hire a rickshaw or taxi to reach the Taj Mahal from the station.

Cultural Tips

Dress modestly when visiting religious sites. It's customary to remove your shoes before entering the Taj Mahal.

×

Visitor Details for Taj Mahal

Entry fee: ₹50 (Indians), ₹1300 (Foreigners).

Opening hours: 6 AM - 7 PM daily.

Great Wall of China

A marvel of ancient architecture.

×

Local Tips for Great Wall of China

Best time to visit: Spring and autumn for pleasant weather.

Nearby attractions: Ming Tombs, Summer Palace.

Local Food

Try Peking Duck, Dim Sum, and the famous Chinese dumplings when visiting Beijing.

Transport

The Great Wall can be accessed from Beijing by bus or train. You can also hire a private car or take a guided tour.

Cultural Tips

When in China, use both hands to offer or receive something from someone, as it is seen as a sign of respect.

×

Visitor Details for Great Wall of China

Entry fee: ¥40-¥60 (Varies by section).

Opening hours: 8 AM - 6 PM daily.
