Featured Attractions

Eiffel Tower

Located in Paris, the Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world. Known for its stunning architectural design and breathtaking views of the city, it is a must-see attraction for any traveler.

The Eiffel Tower, built in 1889, stands 324 meters tall and offers a 360-degree view of Paris. Visitors can take an elevator or climb the stairs to reach the observation decks.

It is one of the most visited paid monuments in the world, with more than 7 million visitors annually.

Taj Mahal

A symbol of love and a masterpiece of Mughal architecture, the Taj Mahal is located in Agra, India. This white marble mausoleum is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and attracts millions of visitors every year.

Completed in 1653, the Taj Mahal is considered the jewel of Muslim art in India. It is known for its intricate carvings, beautiful gardens, and reflection pool that adds to its elegance.

It is best visited in the early morning to avoid crowds, and visitors can enjoy the sight of the monument bathed in the warm glow of the sunrise.

Great Wall of China

One of the greatest man-made structures, the Great Wall of China stretches over 13,000 miles. It was originally built to protect China from invasions, and today it offers stunning views and an opportunity to experience ancient history.

Construction of the Great Wall started in the 7th century BC and continued until the 17th century. The wall offers magnificent panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes and attracts thousands of hikers and tourists every year.

Visitors can explore sections of the wall, like Badaling and Mutianyu, where the wall has been well-preserved and is easily accessible.
